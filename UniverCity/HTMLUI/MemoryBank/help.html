Private Journal

The private journal allows you to easily and securely document your daily college experience. You can also use it to take notes, remember a phone number, or just jot down your thoughts for the day.

My Notifications

This is where you receive your online coupons, deals, and notices from retailers offering special deals just for you!

Photos/Videos

This allows you to take photos or videos from Facebook and other social network accounts and embed them into your private journal. You can also take a picture or video directly from your mobile device and embed them into your journal.

Preferences

This allows you to choose your preferences based upon your personal interests and receive deals from retailers and event invitations from other student members based upon these choices.
