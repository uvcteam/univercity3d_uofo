Welcome

Welcome University of Oregon students to the UniverCity3D application. Below you will find a list of what the different sections of the application can do for you.

  •  Virtual Mall

    • The virtual mall is your location to find exclusive deals at local businesses. Craving pizza or perhaps looking to save on gym membership? Check here!

  •  Union Hall - must be logged in

    • Union hall is a destination offered exclusively to students. Here you can create events or find events that interest you. Need to study or perhaps trying to find a group with similar hobbies as yourself? Look no further.

  •  Memory Bank - must be logged in

    • Memory bank is another student exclusive place where you can manage things like your personal journals as well as different deals from businesses that correlate to the categories that the you are interested in.

  •  Explorer

    • Explorer is where you can look at a 3D representation of Eugene and see where your favorite places and businesses are. You can also view the mega deals or discounts for your favorite places here!

  •  Arcade - coming soon!

    • Arcade is where you can play quick games to earn Ducats. With these Ducats you can purchase things at your favorite businesses. Real rewards for playing games? We think yes, don't you?
